About Task Monster
What is Task Monster?
Hi and welcome to Task Monster, a task manager / monster evolution application. You can call it hybrid application that will help you remeber and finish your tasks and in return, have some fun.

What's difference?
Task monster allow you to chose among five monsters (different race) which are Pongo, Fluffy, Happy, Lemono and Zooka.

Pongo

Hyperactive and very silly!

Fluffy

Loves his peace and freedom!

Happy

Grumpy, but loves pizza!

Lemono

Bitter crazy monster!

Zooka

Sick of tasks, feed him!
And I can do what?
First time you register, you can chose name and race of your monster so chose well, according to your likings!

Task mosters are actually chewing tasks and when you finish task by clicking on (X) on your top right corner of task window, your feeding your monster. By doing that, you moster will grow and get new titles.

Is that all?
There is also Hall Of Fame, a special place where you can find the most hard working monsters from all registered users. Again, your finished tasks will influence the position of your monster on the list.
Please note that if you don't chose a monster before you start your tasks, tasks prior to chosen monster will not be counted into Hall of Fame list.

When it comes to the monster titles, you can attain : Youngling, Hunter, Predator, Lord, King and Emperor. Now, it's up to you to discover them!
